Hakka Noodles

Ingredients:

  • Utensils use Frying pan and Bowl


  • 300g Noodles


  • 400g paneer


  • Red chilli paste


  • Vinegar and Soya Sauce


  • Oil


  • vegetables


  • Salt and Ajinomoto to taste


Instructions:

  1. Boil a large pot of water and cook the noodles according to the instructions.

  2. Afterwards let the noodles to be dry for some time

  3. In a separate pan, cook the paneer pieces, onion, and vegetables until the paneer is browned and the onion is soft.

  4. Add the soya sauce, vinegar, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper to the pan containing noodles and the vegies. Stir well and let it cook for 5 minutes.

  5. Serve the cooked noodles in a bowl accordingly.
